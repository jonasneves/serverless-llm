Variations: Mitigate Mode Collapse

The Problem: Post-training alignment (RLHF) causes LLMs to suffer from "mode collapse" - they give the same predictable response every time due to typicality bias.

The Solution: Verbalized Sampling asks for a distribution of responses instead of one response. This forces the model to tap into its diverse pre-trained knowledge rather than the narrow post-aligned mode.
